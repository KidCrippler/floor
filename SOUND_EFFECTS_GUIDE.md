## üéØ Quick Start - Get All Sounds in 10 Minutes

### Option 1: Freesound.org (Best Quality, Free, CC Licensed)

**Visit:** https://freesound.org

1. **Create a free account** (required to download)
2. Search for each sound using the terms below
3. **Filter by:** License: "Creative Commons 0" (no attribution needed)
4. **Sort by:** Rating or Downloads
5. Download as MP3

#### Exact Search Terms & What to Look For:

**countdown.mp3** - Search: "countdown 3 2 1 go" or "countdown voice"
- Duration: 3-4 seconds
- Look for: Complete countdown sequence with "3, 2, 1, GO!" or similar
- Recommended: Voice countdown with energetic finish

**correct.mp3** - Search: "correct answer" or "success chime"
- Duration: 0.3-0.8 seconds
- Look for: Positive, rewarding sound
- Recommended: "Success Ding", "Correct Bell", "Achievement"

**skip.mp3** - Search: "wrong answer" or "error buzz"
- Duration: 0.3-0.8 seconds
- Look for: Negative but not too harsh
- Recommended: "Wrong Buzz", "Error Beep", "Deny"

**gameover.mp3** - Search: "victory fanfare" or "game end"
- Duration: 1-3 seconds
- Look for: Celebratory or dramatic ending
- Recommended: "Victory Jingle", "Game Win", "Success Fanfare"

**background.mp3** - Search: "upbeat game music loop"
- Duration: 2-5 minutes
- Look for: Energetic, not too distracting, loops well
- Recommended: "Game Background", "Trivia Music", "Quiz Show"
